Design

A chair for musicians, designed to improve posture and performance

To design earó, Dr Gearóid Ó Conchubhair, consulted with 250 orchestras around the world. Inspired by musicians' insights, earó embodies 4 key principles: Stability, Mobility, Flexibility and Longevity.

The Chair

It’s time to improve your work-life balance

earó is a chair design for musicians and their specific requirements. It’s unique equatilt principle allow musicians maintain their posture for prolonged periods of time while keeping them supported to reduce muscle fatigue.
